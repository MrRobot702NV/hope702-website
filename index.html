Our Mission
"Nobody should die on a Las Vegas sidewalk because they didn't know where to go."

Built in Las Vegas, Nevada

Las Vegas summers reach 115°F. For the thousands of people living unsheltered in the valley, that's not a weather report — it's a matter of survival. HOPE 702 was built by a local who got tired of watching people suffer steps away from resources they didn't know existed.

We built a single, simple tool: text the word HOPE to our number and receive an instant list of the closest cooling centers, shelters, food distributions, water stations, and pet-friendly resources near you. No app. No internet. No account. Just a text.

We believe access to emergency resources shouldn't require a smartphone, Wi-Fi, or knowing the right people. It should take five seconds and a basic cell phone — because that's often all someone in crisis has.

Simple by Design

How HOPE 702 Works

Three steps. Any phone. Any time. Because help shouldn't be complicated.

1

Text HOPE

Send the word HOPE to our number from any cell phone — no data plan, no smartphone required. Standard SMS only.

Also works with:
SHELTER WATER FOOD PET ALL
2

Share Your Location

Reply with your zip code or cross streets. Our system instantly matches you to the nearest open resources in real time.

No location? Just say the area you're in — downtown, east side, north Vegas — and we'll do our best.
3

Get Help Immediately

Receive a text back within seconds listing open facilities, distances, hours, and whether they accept pets — updated daily.
